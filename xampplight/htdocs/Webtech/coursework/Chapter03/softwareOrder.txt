This program will calculate the total cost of Microsoft Word license keys.
Lets say that a license key is $20,000 (pretty close!)

softwareOrder.html algorithm:

	Prompt for softwareAmount
	Get softwareAmount
	Submit softwareAmount to softwareOrder.php
END

softwareOrder.php algorithm:

	Recieve softwareAmount
	totalCost = softwareAmount * 20000
	Display softwareAmount, totalCost
END
