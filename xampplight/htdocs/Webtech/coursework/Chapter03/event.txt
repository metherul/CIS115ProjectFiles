This program will calculate the total cost of your party based on the amount of adults and children that come.
	
event.html algorithm:

	Prompt amountAdults
	Get amountAdults
	Prompt amountChildren
	Get amountChildren
	Submit amountAdults, amountChildren to event.php
END

event.php algorithm:

	Recieve amountAdults, amountChildren
	adultCost = amountAdults * 5.32
	childCost = amountChildren * 9.98
	totalCost = adultCost + childCost
	Display childCost, adultCost, totalCost
END
