Fuel cost: $2.00.
This program calculates the total fuel cost - find by multiplying the cost of the Fuel by the total amount bought.

fuelCost.html algorithm:

	Prompt for the total amount of Fuel purchased
	Get fuelAmount
	Submit fuelAmount to fuelCost.php	
END

fuelCost.php algorithm:

	Recieve fuelAmount from fuelCost.php
	fuelCost = $2.00 #Note that in normal programming you cannot use the dollar symbol.
	totalFuelCost = fuelCost * fuelAmount
	Display fuelAmount, fuelCost, totalFuelCost

END
